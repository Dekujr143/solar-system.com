SOLAR SYSTEM (Star System)


-The Solar System is the gravitationally bound system of the Sun and the objects that orbit it. The Solar System formed 4.6 billion years ago from the gravitational collapse of a giant interstellar molecular cloud.



Age: 4.571 billion years

Known minor planets: 1,199,224

Known dwarf planets: 9 by general consensus: Ceres; Orcus; Pluto; Haumea; Quaoar; Makemake; Gonggong; Eris; Sedna

Nearest known planetary system: Proxima Centauri system (4.2441 ly)
